## Key UX Issues Observed
- Too many notifications can distract users.
- Cart access is not always intuitive after adding products.
- First-time users may feel overwhelmed due to many features on the home screen.

## Suggested Improvements
- Provide a short onboarding guide for first-time users.
- Reduce notification frequency and allow better customization.
- Improve cart visibility after adding items.
- Make navigation between pages smoother and faster.

## Learning Outcome
This analysis helped me understand how user experience impacts engagement, retention, and usability in large-scale applications.
